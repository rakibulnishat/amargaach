How আমারগাছ Works

Four simple steps from choosing your tree to tasting its fruit — we handle everything in between.

01
🌱

Choose Your Tree

Pick a fruit variety and package. We assign you a real, named tree on our partner farms in Rajshahi, Dinajpur, or Sylhet.

Regular video and photo updates of your tree through the season. See it blossom, fruit, and ripen in real time.
